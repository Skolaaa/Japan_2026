# Japan Winter Adventure 2025 🗾

An interactive travel map showcasing my 3-week winter journey through Japan from January 18 to February 7, 2025.

## 🗺️ Live Map
Visit the interactive map: [https://yourusername.github.io/japan-travel-map](https://yourusername.github.io/japan-travel-map)

## 📍 Itinerary Overview

### Week 1: Tokyo Discovery (Days 1-7)
- **Tokyo** - Base for modern culture, temples, and city exploration
- **Nikko** - UNESCO World Heritage temples and winter scenery
- **Hakone** - Hot springs and Mount Fuji views
- **Kamakura** - Great Buddha and traditional zen culture

### Week 2: Japanese Alps & Snow Country (Days 8-12)
- **Nagano** - Famous snow monkeys and mountain hot springs
- **Matsumoto** - Historic black castle and traditional crafts
- **Takayama** - Traditional architecture and Hida beef
- **Shirakawa-go** - UNESCO World Heritage thatched-roof village

### Week 3: Kansai Culture & Tokyo Shopping (Days 13-21)
- **Kyoto** - Traditional temples, gardens, and geisha districts
- **Nara** - Ancient capital with friendly deer and Setsubun festival
- **Osaka** - Food paradise with street food culture
- **Tokyo Return** - Shopping finale in Ginza and Harajuku

## 📱 Customization

### Adding Your Own Destinations:
1. Edit `assets/js/travel-data.js`
2. Update coordinates using [LatLong.net](https://www.latlong.net/)
3. Modify colors, descriptions, and highlights
4. Commit changes to see them live

### Changing Colors:
1. Edit CSS custom properties in `assets/css/styles.css`
2. Update `TRAVEL_DATA` color values in `travel-data.js`

### Adding Photos:
1. Create an `assets/images/` folder
2. Add photos and reference them in popup content
3. Keep images under 1MB for fast loading
